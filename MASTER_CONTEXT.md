# Master Context

## Business

SignalLabs helps owner-led service businesses recover leads and remove recurring administrative work using the systems they already have. The immediate acquisition niche is independent gyms and owner-led fitness businesses.

The offer should feel practical and non-technical. We are not selling “AI transformation.” We are finding one revenue leak or repeated workflow, installing the smallest useful fix, and proving the value.

## Current offer ladder

1. **Free Mini Assessment** — 15 minutes to find the first obvious opportunity.
2. **Full Systems Audit** — $999. Inspect one workflow, recommend/configure 3–7 targeted tools or automations when appropriate, and target at least five hours per week of recurring work removed.
3. **Concierge Implementation** — starts at $1,200/month for ongoing buildout and maintenance.

## Current positioning

- Recover missed calls, slow replies, and forgotten follow-up.
- Remove repeated computer/admin work.
- Keep useful tools already in place.
- Preserve owner approval for messages, promises, and material changes.
- Start narrow enough to judge and measure.

## Gym-specific wedge

Lead with revenue and operational leaks gyms immediately understand:

- missed calls and delayed callbacks;
- unworked web/social leads;
- trial-pass leads that never receive structured follow-up;
- membership inquiries cooling off;
- old leads never reactivated;
- no-show and appointment follow-up;
- repetitive front-desk questions;
- inconsistent review/referral requests;
- owner/manager time trapped in inboxes, spreadsheets, and staff handoffs.

The likely first productized wedge is **Gym Lead Recovery**: capture inquiries from existing channels, make the next action visible, draft rapid follow-up, preserve human approval where needed, and report whether prospects were contacted and booked.
