# Fable 5 Revenue Sprint Briefs

Public, sanitized collaboration space for two short Fable 5 sprints supporting SignalLabs and F10.0RD.

## Primary objective

Land as many qualified gym clients for SignalLabs as quickly as responsibly possible.

## Allocation

- 8 Fable calls per day: SignalLabs gym-client acquisition.
- 2 Fable calls per day: F10.0RD conversion and growth.
- Two-day total: 16 SignalLabs calls, 4 F10.0RD calls.

## Collaboration model

Fable is reached by text through a Google Voice number and should not receive credentials, private client data, internal logs, or secrets. Each call prompt is self-contained and requests a concrete artifact. Quan can forward Fable's reply to Botler, who will save, synthesize, and turn it into the next execution artifact.

## Boundaries

- No secrets, credentials, private customer data, lead phone lists, or internal system paths in this repository.
- No claims of results that have not happened.
- No autonomous outreach, purchases, publishing, account creation, or production deployment.
- Strategy must optimize for gym-client acquisition, not generic AI-agency theory.
